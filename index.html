How Much is Your HDB Flat Valued ?

This project is focused on exploring influential factors on HDB resale prices and predicting price trends.

Background

Singapore's Housing and Development Board (HDB) is a government agency responsible for providing affordable public housing to Singaporeans. About 80% of Singapore's resident population lives in HDB flats, making the HDB resale market a significant component of the country's housing landscape.

Analyzing the HDB resale market provides valuable insights for policymakers, buyers, and sellers in making informed decisions and understanding the dynamics of Singapore's public housing landscape.

ABOUT THIS PROJECT

Introduction

In this project, we will focus on analyzing the resale prices of Singapore Housing and Development Board (HDB) flats. We will perform exploratory data analysis (EDA) and confirmatory data analysis, presenting the findings in an interactive Shiny app. Additionally, we will build a predictive model utilizing relevant factors to help customers estimate the resale price of HDB flats based on their preferences.

Univariate Analysis

univariate plots help understand the distribution, frequency, and range of values for each variable. This analysis can provide insights into the characteristics of the HDB flats in the dataset and help identify any outliers or unusual patterns.

Bivariate and Correlation Analysis

The correlation analysis provides valuable information about the relationships between variables and can guide feature selection and model building decisions in the HDB resale price prediction project. Variables with strong correlations can be considered as potential predictors in the predictive model.

Predictive Modelling

The prediction model provides an interactive interface for users to easily leverage a real estate price prediction model. By translating user-inputted property parameters into estimated resale prices, it allows for convenient and quick property valuation estimates that could help inform a variety of real estate decisions and analysis.
